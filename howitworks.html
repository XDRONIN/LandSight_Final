How It Works

Discover how Landsight tracks Landsat 9, predicts satellite passes, and provides real-time data access to empower your environmental monitoring and research.

Get Started

Begin your journey by navigating to the main map view page. Easily access the interactive map to initiate your satellite tracking experience.

Select Your Location

Select any location on the map by clicking on it or entering the longitude and latitude coordinates. View the selected area with a detailed 3x3 grid for precise monitoring.

Get Next Approach Time

After submitting your selected location, receive the next approach time and date/time of the Landsat 9 satellite. Stay informed about upcoming satellite passes over your area.

Receive Notifications

Stay ahead with timely notifications about the satellite's approach time. Set time to when you want to receive alerts before the Landsat 9 passes over your selected location.

Download Live Data

Access and download near real-time Surface Reflectance (SR) data from Landsat 9 in TIF format. Utilize high-resolution imagery and environmental data for your research and monitoring needs.

Track Landsat 9, predict satellite passes, and unlock real-time data for your location. Empower your environmental monitoring and research with Landsight.
